M.Sc. Thesis · 2025-2026

Failure Analysis of Medical Vision-Language Models

A complete evaluation pipeline for BiomedCLIP, MedCLIP, MedKLIP, and CLIP-based baselines across medical image classification and biomedical figure-caption matching tasks.

  • Compared in-domain and out-of-domain performance across PMC figure-caption data, CheXpert, and NIH ChestX-ray14.
  • Analyzed Atelectasis, Cardiomegaly, Consolidation, Edema, and Pleural Effusion classification behavior.
  • Studied AUROC, AUPRC, F1, fixed-specificity sensitivity, Expected Calibration Error, thresholds, and bootstrap confidence intervals.
  • Investigated prompt sensitivity, prompt ensembling, CoOp, CoCoOp, VPT, and MaPLe prompt-learning degradation.
